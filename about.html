Our Journey

Built on Passion for Technology

Circuit Czar was born out of a simple frustration: the electronics market was full of overpriced, unreliable services and mediocre products. We set out to change that.

Founded by a team of certified engineers and tech enthusiasts, Circuit Czar started as a small repair workshop in Karachi. Word spread quickly — not just about our technical skill, but about our honesty, transparency, and the genuine care we put into every device.

Today, we've grown into a full-service electronics destination offering premium device sales, expert repairs, smart home solutions, and custom PC builds — all under one roof and all backed by our uncompromising quality guarantee.
